# 滴天髓：气势层

Use this reference in Step 4.

## Purpose

《滴天髓》 is the气机 audit. It asks whether the chart is alive, blocked, clear, muddy, flowing, fighting, too cold, too hot, too dry, or too wet.

Do not reduce this layer to element counts.

## Audit Handles

### 体用

- 体 is the chart's body:日主、形象、气局、dominant structure.
- 用 is the operating pivot that lets the body function.
- If body and use are both strong but disconnected, the chart can still be stuck.

### 衰旺

- 月令 matters, but month command alone is not enough.
- Judge root, support, drain, control, transformation, and seasonal进退.
- Extreme strength may need泄 or顺势, not direct克.
- Extreme weakness may need从势, not ordinary扶抑.

### 中和

- 中和 is not equal element quantity.
- It means the chart's tensions are proportioned and usable.
- A biased chart can work if its bias is clear and luck cycles安顿 it.

### 源流

- Trace where the energy starts, how it moves, and where it ends.
- A useful final outlet can turn pressure into achievement.
- A blocked or polluted outlet can make an otherwise strong chart hard to express.

### 通关

- Use when two forces fight and a third force can mediate.
- 通关 must actually connect both sides by position, root, visibility, or luck cycle.
- Do not invent通关 from an element that is absent or too weak.

### 清浊

- 清 means the useful structure is ordered and not polluted by close enemies.
- 浊 means mixed, blocked, grabbed, or confused.
- 清 but weak is still limited;浊 but removable can improve in the right luck cycle.

### 真神假神

- 真神 is the real seasonal or structural force.
- 假神 can still be usable if the chart and luck support it.
- Do not let a flashy exposed stem override the real command of the chart.

### 寒暖燥湿

- This overlaps with调候 but is broader.
- Judge whether the whole chart can generate life, not only whether one medicine is present.

## Pitfalls

- Do not count elements and call that气势.
- Do not average contradictions. A strong poison is not “medium”; it is strong and poisonous unless transformed.
- Do not ignore luck cycles. Some charts only become clean when the right cycle removes blockage.
- Do not overuse special structures; first ask whether the normal flow already explains the chart.
