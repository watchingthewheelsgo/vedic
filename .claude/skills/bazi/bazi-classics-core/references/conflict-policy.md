# 三层冲突裁决

Use this reference in Step 5.

## Principle

The three classics answer different questions. Conflict is expected.

| Layer | Question | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 穷通宝鉴 | What climate medicine is needed? | 调候用神 |
| 子平真诠 | What structural pattern is being protected? | 格局用神、相神、忌神 |
| 滴天髓 | How does the energy move or block? | 气势、通关、中和、清浊 |

## Priority Rules

1. **Life-support first**: if the chart is too cold, hot, dry, or wet to function,调候 must be addressed before praising a pattern.
2. **Pattern integrity second**: once climate can function, protect the格局用神 and相神.
3. **Flow decides expression**: a technically good pattern with blocked source-flow-outlet expresses poorly until通关 or清浊 improves.
4. **Luck changes priority**: a luck cycle may reveal medicine, damage the helper, clear mud, or trigger war. State which layer the luck cycle affects.
5. **No silent merge**: never write “喜用火土” unless you explain whether火 is调候、格局、通关, and whether土 is helper, wealth, disease, or mediator.

## Conflict Examples

### Climate wants fire, pattern fears fire

Say:

> 调候层需要火来暖局，但格局层忌火伤官/破印。火不能简单视为全局喜神；它需要有位置、强度和制化，能暖而不破格才好。

### Pattern wants官杀, body is too weak

Say:

> 格局层有官杀可用，但气势层显示日主承载不足。先看印比或通关能否承接官杀，否则官杀成压力而非秩序。

### 身旺宜泄, but source-flow is blocked

Say:

> 不是见食伤就好。食伤若被枭夺、被合绊、无财承接，泄气不成出口，反成消耗。
