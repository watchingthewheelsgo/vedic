# 子平真诠：格局层

Use this reference in Step 3.

## Purpose

《子平真诠》 centers the report on 月令、用神、相神、成败、救应、行运. Its “用神” is a structural pivot, not the same thing as the climate medicine from 《穷通宝鉴》.

## Order of Judgment

1. Start from month command: 月令 is the纲.
2. Identify the monthly human element and what透干.
3. Determine whether the chart forms a standard pattern,禄劫/月劫,阳刃,从化, or a special structure.
4. Define:
   - 用神：the structural pivot of the pattern
   - 相神：the helper that protects or completes the use
   - 忌神：the factor that breaks the use
   - 救应：the factor that removes disease or restores the pattern
5. Judge成、败、成中有败、败中有成.
6. Judge luck cycles by whether they护格、破格、变格、救应.

## Standard Pattern Handles

- 正官：喜财印相随，忌伤官破官、官杀混杂、官星被合去。
- 财：喜食神生财、财生官，忌比劫夺财；财印能否并用 depends on placement.
- 印：喜官杀生印 or 食伤泄身 depending on weight;忌财破印 unless arranged as救应.
- 食神：喜身旺生食、食神生财；忌枭神夺食.
- 七杀：身强杀旺喜食神制杀；杀印相生 also possible;忌财党杀无制.
- 伤官：身强可伤官生财，身弱可伤官佩印；伤官见官 is not a blanket rule and must be judged by structure.
- 阳刃：喜官杀制刃，财印可佐;忌无制或制化失当.
- 建禄/月劫：月令 itself is usually not the use; seek财官杀食 in the rest of the chart.

## 成败救应 Checklist

For every proposed pattern:

1. Is the use clear or mixed?
2. Is the use rooted and protected?
3. Is the helper close enough to help?
4. Is the enemy close enough to break?
5. Is there a rescue that removes the enemy?
6. Does the rescue create a new disease?
7. Does the current luck cycle reveal, protect, or damage the use?

## Pitfalls

- Do not use “身强身弱” as the only gate. Month command and pattern integrity come first.
- Do not call every helpful element “用神”. Keep 用神、相神、喜神、救应 distinct.
- Do not treat神煞 as pattern evidence. They can be minor notes, not the core judgment.
- Do not mechanically reject “伤官见官”; judge whether the structure has财、印、制化 or special climate needs.
